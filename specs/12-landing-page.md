## Landing Page
This is the landing page for the OpenModels platform. 

- It will be same as out full app ui, just not signed in and instead of settings button their will be Sign Up and Login buttons
- And when clickng on it will redirect to /auth page
- when user is signed in, than our normal ui shows jsut those buttons are gone and settings icon will be back
- If user is not signed in, he can only send 5 msg and also they were not saved anywhere just for that browser session
- When the 5 msg are completed than it will show a pop up to sign up or login to continue using.
